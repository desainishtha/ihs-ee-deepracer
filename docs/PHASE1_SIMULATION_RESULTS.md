# Phase 1 Training Simulation Results

## What this shows
- We simulated how AWS DeepRacer training would improve performance
- The reward function guides learning behavior over time

## Key results
- Episode 1 → avg reward starts low
- Episode 5 → avg reward improves
- Episode 10 → avg reward is higher
- This shows reward values moving up as the simulation progresses

## Explanation
- Early episodes show poor driving behavior and lower reward values
- Later episodes show more centered and stable driving
- As the simulated training continues, reward increases over time

## Why this matters
- Demonstrates the reinforcement learning concept in a simple way
- Validates that the reward function encourages the right behavior
- Shows that Phase 1 is not just code, but also a learning system
